Outreach Sequences & Tactics

Crafted messages for email and LinkedIn designed to build curiosity and relationships.

Step 1: The Trigger Email

Subject: Unlock ROI from your ERP with AI automation

Hey {{first_name}},

Noticed {{company_name}} is scaling in the {{industry}} space. Many growing teams using Dynamics 365 Business Central struggle to unlock full ROI — especially with outdated workflows or fragmented tools.

Our clients in retail & wholesale have increased ops efficiency by 25%+ by automating workflows with Business Central customizations and AI add-ons.

Would it be a bad idea to show you how we’ve done it?

Step 2: The Value Add

Subject: Quick use case: Business Central + AI

Thought I’d share a quick example:

One client in retail automated 70% of their manual invoice reconciliation with a Business Central extension — saving 12+ hours/week for their accounting team.

If you’re open to seeing how this works, I can send over a few examples or book a quick call.

Let me know,

Step 3: The Breakup

Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi {{first_name}},

Haven’t heard back — totally fine if now’s not the right time.

Just wanted to check in before I close this out. If improving how you use Dynamics 365 BC is on the radar later this quarter, I’d be happy to reconnect.

Best,

Context: Connection Request

Context: Day 2 after connection

Step 3: Light CTA

Context: Day 4 after connection

No pitch — just insights from other {{industry}} teams using BC.

Next Steps & Execution Plan

This playbook is designed to be a living document for the NavisionTech outbound team. To get started, the first step is to set up and validate the technology stack and data sources.

Phase 1: Setup

  • Onboard team to Instantly.ai and Prosp.ai.
  • Configure n8n for lead scraping automation.
  • Purchase and warm up mailboxes and domains.

Phase 2: Launch

  • Scrape first 10,000 leads and clean the list.
  • Launch cold email campaign with Step 1 message.
  • Begin LinkedIn outreach with soft connect messages.

Phase 3: Optimize

  • Track key metrics (open rate, reply rate, show-up rate).
  • A/B test subject lines and copy.
  • Integrate CRM to track deal flow and ROI.
